Formación Academica de Eduardo Garea-Llano

Eduardo Garea Llano was born on April 6, 1965 in Havana, Cuba. He obtained the title of Mining Engineer, Geologist and Hydrogeologist and M.Sc. in Geology at the Mining University of Saint Petersburg , Russia in 1993 and the Scientific Degree of Doctor in Technical Sciences at the “José Martí” Military Technical Institute of Havana in 2003.
